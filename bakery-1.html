Our Story

Baked the honest way, every single day.

La Miche was born from a simple obsession: making bread that actually tastes like bread. No improvers, no shortcuts — just flour, water, salt, and time.

Our head baker, Margot, trained in Lyon before bringing her craft back home. Every loaf, every pastry, every tart is made by hand, in small batches, before sunrise.
